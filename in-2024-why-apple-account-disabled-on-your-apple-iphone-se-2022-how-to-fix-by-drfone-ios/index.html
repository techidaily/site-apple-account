Occasionally, macOS may encounter bugs or glitches that prevent you from logging out of your Apple ID. These issues can usually be resolved with software updates or troubleshooting steps.

Solution

To resolve the bugs and glitches within the MacBook, the best option is to update macOS to its latest version. For that, access “System Settings” through Dock and move to the “General” tab. Here, select “Software Update“ to verify if an update is available and if an update is available, use “Update Now” to update the macOS.
