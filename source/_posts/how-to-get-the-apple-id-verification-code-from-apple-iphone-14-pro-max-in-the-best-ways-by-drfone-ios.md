- Open your Apple iPhone 14 Pro Max's **Settings**.

- You'll be asked to enter your Apple ID passcode and tap **Turn Off.**
- Confirm by selecting the data you want to keep on your Apple iPhone 14 Pro Max.
- Finally, tap **Sign Out.**
